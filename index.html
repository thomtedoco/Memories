Milestones and Memories

Montmorency Falls

There we were, standing hand in hand at Montmorency Falls, the mist kissing our faces as the water roared behind us. Surrounded by family, laughter echoed louder than the falls themselves. We weren’t just admiring the view—we were part of it. Every smile, every playful shove, and every goofy photo captured more than just a moment; it captured the love and chaos that makes family so unforgettable. And somewhere between dodging water spray and sharing snacks, we realized this was more than just a trip—it was a beautiful chapter in our story

First Christmas together with her Family

Our first Christmas at her house was filled with love and warmth. Diana was happily caring for baby Carli, while daddy Ariel is always hungry. Marilou’s kind presence made me feel welcome, and Jessa, despite always being busy with calls, joined in to celebrate.Tita Elsa prepared the most delicious food, bringing everyone together. Being surrounded by her family, I felt truly grateful. In that moment, I knew I was exactly where I was meant to be..
